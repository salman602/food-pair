About Us

Safe food is our basic human right. Safe and healthy foods are becoming scarce and inaccessible commodities in Bangladesh as the dependencies towards unhealthy ways of cultivation and preservation are on the rise. Nowadays, our palates have evolved to eat much more adventurously than before, with unexpected ingredient combinations becoming the new norm. As chefs and bartenders, this presents us with exciting new challenges and a whole world of creative opportunities.
